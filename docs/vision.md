\# FitStore AI — Product Vision



\## Target User

People who want fitness, nutrition, and shopping handled in a single

application — instead of juggling separate apps for workouts, diet tracking,

and buying supplements/gear. They value convenience and hate app-switching.



\## Core Value Proposition

One fast, easy-to-use app that combines shopping and fitness guidance —

plus AI-driven personalization that generic fitness apps and generic

stores don't offer on their own.





\## MVP Features

1\. \*\*Browse/search products\*\* — core catalog browsing and search

2\. \*\*AI-curated recommendations\*\* — personalized product suggestions based on

&#x20;  user goals/profile (the key differentiator vs. plain e-commerce apps)

3\. \*\*Cart\*\* — add/remove/update items before purchase

4\. \*\*Checkout\*\* — complete a purchase end-to-end

5\. \*\*Profile + fitness goals\*\* — user sets goals (e.g. weight loss, muscle

&#x20;  gain) which feeds the recommendation engine

6\. \*\*Order history\*\* — view past purchases, enables repeat-buy convenience





\## One-line differentiation

FitStore AI is the all-in-one fitness ecosystem that brings together workout tracking, running analytics, food scanning, progress monitoring, and fitness shopping into a single platform. By eliminating the need for multiple apps, reducing inaccurate data, and removing intrusive advertisements, FitStore AI delivers a smarter, more trustworthy fitness experience.
